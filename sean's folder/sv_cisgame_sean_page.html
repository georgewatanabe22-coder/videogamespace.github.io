Orbie's Adventures

Game Overview

Orbie’s Adventures is a cheerful and cartoony action puzzle platformer great for people of all ages. For each level, guide Orbie to their objective, helping Orbie explore, navigate mazes, collect cubes, utilize random mechanics, and avoid enemies. (Eek, Scary!)

Game Features

  • Compatable for both desktop and mobile!
  • Simple controls to guide Orbie (Arrow keys and WASD for desktop, JoyStick and Jump Button for mobile)
  • Each Level offers a completely new map!
  • Overall Great Replayability!

About the Developer

Sean von Wolffradt is a novice game developer, so if this game looks one of his first, that's because it is. He hopes that with a little more time, he will be able to implement all the mechanics and levels he has planned, including:

  • Moving Walls
  • Rising and lowering platforms
  • Other characters besides Orbie and Enemies
  • The most Awesome 3d maze
  • A ton of power-ups, from super speed, to giant strength, to itty bitty, to invisibility.
  • And a super cute main character (Alright, not a mechanic or level, but still)
Sean also hopes that in one way or another, he can incorporate his wide variety of interst and knowledge to give the game a fun and quirky nature.

Sean's interest includes chess, D&D, chemistry, Pokemon, nature, spaceships, muthical creatures, and useless triva, so incoporating all of this could be quite a challenge. He also has two cats that he loves very much, Blaze and Leo, as well as parents and siblings, but we don't need to mention them here. Secretly, Sean believes he has super-powers, but "Shh!" that's a secret. (And I don't know why I told you...)
