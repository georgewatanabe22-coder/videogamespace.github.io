American Territory Conqueror


Game Overview

American Territory Conqueror is a strategy map game where the player chooses their state and city to start with, gather resources, build buildings and objects mandatory to their territory, and conquer territories themselves or within an alliance. The important features of this game that would make our game stand out well includes player and computer controlled territories, a set of different types of strategies for conquering, and alliances where multiple territories can team up together in each alliance. There is no storyline in the game but the player's or alliance's main goal is to conquer all territories of the 50 U.S. states.

Key Game Features

  • Player's state and city choice - When starting a new game, the player has a choose to choose a state and a city. The game comes with 50 states whereas depending on the state, it comes with different types of cities that they have.
  • Grinding resources - When starting a territory with a few people, the player can make choices to gather resources where they can mine more metals, minerals, and fuels. They can also farm more food and materials.
  • Build and craft objects - When the player gathers enough resources for a few items, they can build more buildings, civilian and military equipment, and also routes. They could even craft or invent items with each one with its purpose and effect to their territory.
  • Territory control preparation - Player's should build up their military and political government with methods like solder recruitment, appointing political leader, building military weapons and armor, and also elect a great leader that controls their territory when they're inactive.
  • Conquering with alliances - If the player wanted to join an alliance to make their territory more safer, they can choose any available alliances in the map. If an alliance has one or more player-controlled alliances, the player must press a button that sends a request to join to all other players. With alliances, territories help each other to fight against other alliances or territories.

Game Pitch Details

  • Game Concept - The original game concept is titled American Territory Conqueror and its genre was a strategy map game set in the 50 states of America. The gameplay loop starts after the player chooses their state and city. They start gathering resources from different source locations and then they can start building and crafting their tools, supplies, and buildings for their territory. After that they can set up their military and political systems so that their territory is prepared to conquer. Once they're ready, they can start conquering other territories by themselves or with an alliance. The gameplay loop repeats until all 50 states are conquered by an alliance or a territory and they win the game.
  • Used Assets - The art and visuals of our game is going to look likely similar to a style of 1800s historical America where most of the map, ui, icons, sprites, and other visuals would likely depict a vintage feeling of the 1800s in America. Our characters in our game would look like portraits of real people in the 1800s that shows up in every citizen's status in their territory. A customizable portrait or avatar will be shown in the player's status itself as the territory's leader. Background visuals in our game would look like landscapes and environments of various locations in America during the 1800s. For the background music of the game it is likely composed of classical marches and songs in the 1800s. The mood music of the game could include short classical tunes that play when the player achieved achievements in the game.
